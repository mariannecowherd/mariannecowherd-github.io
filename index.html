Marianne Cowherd

ORCiD

I am a first year PhD student in the UC Berkeley Department of Environmental Science, Policy, and Management, where I study fires and hydrology in the Girotto and Stephens Labs. I graduated from Stanford University in 2019 with a B.S. in Environmental Systems Engineering and an M.S. in Environmental Engineering.

At Stanford, I was advised by Oliver Fringer and Stephen Monismith in the Bob & Norma Street Environmental Fluid Mechanics Laboratory, where I worked on oscillatory wave-current boundary layers.

I enjoy running, backpacking, and making science-themed crosswords. I'm also big on outreach and teaching, which I do at Jasper Ridge Biological Preserve, Outer Coast, and miRcore.
